Stranger Things

Set in the 1980s, "Stranger Things" follows a group of kids in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana, as they encounter supernatural forces and secret government experiments while searching for their missing friend, Will Byers.

Key Information

Genre: Science Fiction, Horror, Mystery

Created by: The Duffer Brothers

Release Date: July 15, 2016

Official Website: Visit Official Site

Cast

  • Winona Ryder as Joyce Byers
  • David Harbour as Jim Hopper
  • Millie Bobby Brown as Eleven
  • Finn Wolfhard as Mike Wheeler
  • Gaten Matarazzo as Dustin Henderson
  • Caleb McLaughlin as Lucas Sinclair

Characters

The series features a diverse cast of characters, including the brave kids who band together to face the unknown, their families, and the mysterious beings from the Upside Down.
