A Perefect Steak recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 good quality steak (ribeye, sirloin, filet mignon, or New York strip – at least 1-inch thick)
  • Kosher or sea salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • High smoke point oil (canola, avocado, or grapeseed)

Cooking Instructions

  1. Bring to Room Temp
    Let the steak sit out for 30–60 minutes before cooking to ensure even doneness.
  2. Dry and Season
    Pat the steak completely dry with paper towels. Generously season both sides with salt and black pepper.
  3. Heat the Pan
    Preheat a cast iron or heavy skillet over high heat until it’s very hot (light smoke is okay).Sear the Steak
  4. Add Flavor (Optional)
    Lower heat to medium. Add butter, garlic, and herbs. Tilt the pan and spoon the melted butter over the steak for 1–2 minutes.

  6. Rest the Steak
    Remove the steak from the pan and let it rest for 5–10 minutes to retain juices.
  7. Slice and Serve
    Slice against the grain and serve with your favorite sides.
